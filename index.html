Research

My research interest lie in AI, NLP, Human AI interaction, aiming to build AI systems that can interpret nuance, understand human behaviour, and align with diverse human requirements.

  • How can AI-driven systems effectively collaborate with users?
  • How to design NLP models that foster greater inclusivity, particularly for low-resource languages and underrepresented communities?
